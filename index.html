St. Theodore Triumphs: Scribe Team Clinches 7th Runner Up at PSPC 2025

By The Scribers | November 20, 2025

TAGUIG CITY — The pens have been put down, and the scores are in. The Theodorian Scribe has officially made its mark on the division level.

Competing against veteran schools across Taguig, our delegates showcased excellence in news writing, editorial cartooning, and layouting during the competition held from November 14–16. Their dedication has brought home the 7th Runner Up distinction.
